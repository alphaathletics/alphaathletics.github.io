About
Matt Irving - Certified Canfitpro Personal Training Specialist

Owner

Matt grew up in the small town of Nelson, BC where he pursued his love of sports, fitness, and the outdoors. After moving to Victoria for school 5 years ago, Matt shifted his mindset on fitness past the gym and started incorporating various bodyweight and sports based conditioning training techniques as well. Through this wide scope of practice, Matt has developed experience and knowledge in many areas of fitness. Through Alpha Athletics Matt hopes to pass on this experience and knowledge to help others attain their own fitness goals and aspirations.
